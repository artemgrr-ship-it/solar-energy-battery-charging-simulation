# solar-energy-battery-charging-simulation
The objective of this project is to design a solar energy battery charge circuit to charge a 48V, 100 Ah Lithium-Ion battery with maximum power from PV array under given solar irradiance conditions. Steps 150 V down to 48 V using a buck converter with P&amp;O MPPT, hitting 99.7% tracking efficiency. Also covers IGBT power losses and thermal design.
